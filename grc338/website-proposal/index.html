Introduction

Chocolate House

Chocolate House is a small chocolate shop located in Denver, Colorado and sells a variety of chocolately goods. Chocolate House is dedicated to offering high quality, ethically sourced and produced chocolates.

Target audience

The people visiting this site are either locals wanting to find out more and visit the shop in real life, or general chocolate lovers looking to make an online purchase.

Those who live in Denver/ have access to visiting the shop in-person are visitng the website to get more information or updates regarding the shop and its products. Those who are not local would use the website to browse the selection and place an online order. Many of Chocolate House's online shoppers are shopping for gifts for holidays or celebrations.
